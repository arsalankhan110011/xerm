Choose the right entry point for your task

The platform is strongest when you move through it deliberately instead of jumping page to page.

01

Screen a location fast

Use Explore when you already know the site, station, place name, or coordinates and need an answer quickly.

  • Search by station, place name, coordinates, or current device location
  • Review KPIs, charts, direction pattern, and recommendation output
  • Adjust scenario controls before building a report
Open Explore
02

Scan the province visually

Use Map when you want to discover promising areas first and then drill into a station from spatial context.

  • Review station positions and heat layers by time slice
  • Spot clusters, outliers, and coverage gaps quickly
  • Jump from marker context into Explore with one click
Open Map
03

Compare candidates and justify decisions

Use Insights and Report when you need rankings, evidence, and a cleaner narrative for presentation or review.

  • Compare annual energy and consistency across stations
  • Generate a report with captured assumptions and visuals
  • Keep the workflow suitable for academic or stakeholder review

Decision Flow

How a strong evaluation should move through WindCast

This keeps the platform aligned with real screening and reporting work instead of only chart browsing.

1

Locate

Start with a known station, typed place, coordinates, or your current device location.

2

Validate

Check mean speed, annual energy, dominant direction, and best-month pattern for the chosen range.

3

Stress-test

Adjust scenario controls to see whether the opportunity still makes sense under realistic losses and hub height.

4

Document

Export charts and generate a report only after the assumptions and nearest-station basis are clear.
